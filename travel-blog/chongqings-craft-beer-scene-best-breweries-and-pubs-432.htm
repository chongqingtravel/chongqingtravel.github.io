Chongqing’s Craft Beer Scene: Best Breweries and Pubs

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Chongqing, a city known for its fiery hotpot and breathtaking mountain views, is also quietly becoming a hotspot for craft beer enthusiasts. With a growing number of breweries and pubs popping up across the city, beer lovers can now enjoy everything from hoppy IPAs to rich stouts while soaking in Chongqing’s vibrant nightlife. Whether you're a local or a traveler looking to explore the city’s lesser-known gems, here’s your guide to the best craft beer spots in Chongqing.

The Rise of Craft Beer in Chongqing

Over the past decade, China’s craft beer movement has gained momentum, and Chongqing is no exception. What started as a niche hobby among expats and local beer aficionados has now evolved into a thriving scene. The city’s unique blend of traditional flavors and modern innovation makes it an exciting place for craft beer experimentation.

Why Chongqing?

Chongqing’s craft beer culture thrives thanks to its:
- Diverse influences: A mix of local and international brewers bringing fresh ideas.
- Nightlife appeal: The city’s lively bar districts provide the perfect backdrop for beer tasting.
- Adventurous palates: Locals who love bold flavors, making Chongqing ideal for experimental brews.

Top Craft Breweries in Chongqing

1. Nanjing Road Brewery

Located in the heart of Jiangbei District, Nanjing Road Brewery is a pioneer in Chongqing’s craft beer scene. Known for its small-batch brews, this spot offers a rotating selection of beers, from crisp lagers to bold barrel-aged stouts.

Must-try beers:
- Mountain Fog IPA: A hazy, tropical IPA with local citrus notes.
- Spicy Chongqing Stout: Infused with Sichuan peppercorns for a fiery kick.

2. Ci Qi Kou Craft Brewing Co.

Nestled near the historic Ci Qi Kou (Ancient Town), this brewery blends tradition with modern brewing techniques. The rustic setting makes it a great stop for tourists exploring Chongqing’s cultural side.

Highlights:
- Ancient Town Ale: A smooth, malt-forward beer with hints of caramel.
- Night Market Porter: Dark and rich, inspired by Chongqing’s street food.

3. Yangtze River Brew House

Overlooking the iconic Yangtze River, this brewery combines great beer with stunning views. Their taproom is a favorite among both locals and expats, offering a relaxed atmosphere perfect for unwinding after a day of sightseeing.

Standout brews:
- Riverside Wheat: A refreshing hefeweizen with subtle banana and clove notes.
- Jialing Juicy NEIPA: A New England-style IPA bursting with juicy hops.

Best Craft Beer Pubs & Taprooms

1. Hopscotch Beer Bar (Yuzhong District)

A cozy, expat-friendly bar with an ever-changing lineup of local and imported craft beers. The knowledgeable staff can guide you through their selection, making it a great spot for beginners and connoisseurs alike.

Why visit?
- Rotating taps featuring rare brews.
- Laid-back vibe with board games and live music nights.

2. The Beer Nest (Jiefangbei)

Situated near Chongqing’s bustling shopping district, The Beer Nest is a hidden gem for beer lovers. Their extensive bottle list includes craft beers from across China and beyond.

Don’t miss:
- Chongqing Mule: A beer cocktail with ginger and lime.
- Weekend tap takeovers with guest breweries.

3. Mountain Town Taproom (Shapingba)

A no-frills taproom with a focus on quality brews. They frequently collaborate with local breweries to create exclusive beers you won’t find elsewhere.

Signature experience:
- Flight tastings to sample multiple beers in one sitting.
- Brewery events with meet-the-brewer nights.

Pairing Beer with Chongqing Cuisine

No visit to Chongqing is complete without indulging in its famous spicy dishes. Here’s how to pair local food with craft beer:

Hotpot & Beer: The Ultimate Combo

  • Malty Amber Ale: Cuts through the heat of Sichuan peppercorns.
  • Crisp Pilsner: Refreshes the palate between spicy bites.

Xiao Mian (Spicy Noodles) & IPA

The bitterness of an IPA balances the intense flavors of Chongqing’s iconic noodles.

Grilled Skewers & Smoked Porter

The smokiness of the beer complements the charred flavors of street BBQ.

Craft Beer Events & Festivals

Chongqing’s beer scene isn’t just about bars—it’s also about community. Keep an eye out for:
- Chongqing Craft Beer Festival (Annual, usually in autumn).
- Brewery tours at local hotspots.
- Tap takeovers where breweries showcase limited-edition releases.

Tips for Beer Travelers

  • Transport: Use Didi or the metro—Chongqing’s hills and bridges make walking between bars a workout.
  • Language: Many craft beer spots have English menus, but learning a few beer-related phrases in Chinese can help.
  • Timing: Evenings (7 PM onwards) are the best time to experience the city’s beer culture.

Chongqing’s craft beer scene is still young but full of potential. Whether you're sipping a river-view brew or diving into a spicy stout, the city offers a unique beer adventure waiting to be explored.
