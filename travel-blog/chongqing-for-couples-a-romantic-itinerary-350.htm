Chongqing for Couples: A Romantic Itinerary

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Chongqing, the "Mountain City," is a dreamy destination for couples seeking adventure, culture, and unforgettable moments. With its misty rivers, dazzling skyline, and spicy-hot cuisine, this metropolis offers a perfect blend of excitement and intimacy. Whether you're strolling through ancient streets or savoring a hotpot dinner with a view, Chongqing promises romance at every turn.

Day 1: Arrival & Riverside Romance

Check-In at a Boutique Hotel

Start your journey by checking into a stylish boutique hotel like NICCOLO Chongqing or The Westin Chongqing Liberation Square. Both offer stunning views of the Yangtze River and the city’s glittering skyline—ideal for a romantic first night.

Sunset at Hongya Cave

Head to Hongya Cave (洪崖洞), a magical stilted village illuminated by golden lights at dusk. Walk hand-in-hand along the wooden walkways, browse quirky shops, and stop for a drink at one of the cliffside bars overlooking the Jialing River.

Dinner with a View

For dinner, reserve a table at Chongqing Binjiang 101, where you can enjoy local dishes like shuizhuyu (spicy boiled fish) while watching boats glide across the river.

Day 2: Cultural Exploration & Hidden Gems

Morning at Ciqikou Ancient Town

Step back in time at Ciqikou (磁器口), a charming Ming-era town with narrow alleys and traditional teahouses. Sip on chongqing cha (local tea) and snack on mala tang (spicy skewers) as you explore artisan shops selling handmade souvenirs.

Love Locks at Liziba Station

Take the light rail to Liziba Station, where couples attach padlocks to the rails as a symbol of eternal love. The nearby Liziba Park offers a quiet escape with lush greenery and panoramic city views.

Afternoon at Eling Park

Escape the hustle at Eling Park (鹅岭公园), a serene hilltop garden with pavilions and winding paths. Climb to the Two Rivers Viewing Pavilion for a breathtaking vista of the Yangtze and Jialing rivers merging.

Hotpot Date Night

No Chongqing trip is complete without a fiery hotpot experience. Book a private booth at Da Long Yi Hotpot (大龙燚) or Xiao Yu Hotpot (晓宇火锅) for a spicy, interactive dining adventure.

Day 3: Adventure & Relaxation

Day Trip to Wulong Karst

Venture out to the Wulong Karst Geological Park, a UNESCO site featured in Transformers 4. Walk across the Glass Skywalk at Tiankeng Difeng (天坑地缝) or take a boat through the eerie Furong Cave. The dramatic landscapes make for epic couple photos!

Soak in a Hot Spring

Unwind at Beibei Hot Springs (北温泉), where mineral-rich waters and private pools create a luxurious retreat. Pair it with a couples’ massage for ultimate relaxation.

Night Cruise on the Yangtze

End the day with a Yangtze River night cruise. Glide past neon-lit bridges like the Chaotianmen Bridge while sipping champagne on deck—pure magic.

Day 4: Modern Chongqing & Farewell

Brunch in Guanyinqiao

Start slow with brunch at Bistro 3 in Guanyinqiao, a trendy district. Try their avocado toast or xiaomian (Chongqing noodles) with a modern twist.

Shopping at IFS Mall

Indulge in retail therapy at Chongqing IFS, home to luxury brands and quirky local designers. Don’t miss the giant panda sculpture on the rooftop!

Last Stop: Jiefangbei

Wrap up your trip at Jiefangbei Square, the heart of Chongqing. Snap a final photo under the People’s Liberation Monument, then grab lazi ji (spicy chicken) from a street vendor for the road.

Chongqing’s mix of old-world charm and futuristic energy makes it a lover’s paradise. From misty mountains to sizzling flavors, every moment here is a chance to fall deeper in love—with the city and each other.
